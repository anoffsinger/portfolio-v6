Hi, I’m Adam—a designer, wannabe engineer, and sometimes writer living in the tree-covered hills of Marin, California.

Right now I work at Patreon, where we help creative folks of all types make money and build fan communities by simply doing what they love. Over the last year or so, I helped redesign our mobile and web products while we rebranded. I’m particularly happy with the new Podcast Player we built as part of that work.

Before joining Patreon, I spent a couple of years at Apple working on the Pay and Wallet team. It was my second time working in fintech. As a long-time Apple fan, it was a dream come true. While I was there, I worked on Tap to Pay and Apple Savings among some other cool things that I hope to see launch someday.

I've worked across a number of different industries, which has taught me that solving problems in new spaces energizes me. I'm also motivated by closing the gap between design and engineering, which has led to a career rooted in more technical parts of design like prototyping, motion design, and design systems. That said, I consider myself a generalist and enjoy wearing many hats.

In my free time, you can find me olympic weightlifting, camping, off-roading in my beloved Defender, or trying to pull the perfect shot of Espresso. On weekends, I build Found —a simple iOS app for tracking your favorite places.
