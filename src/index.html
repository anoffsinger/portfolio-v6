Hi, I’m Adam—a designer, wannabe engineer, and sometimes writer living in the tree-covered hills of Marin, California.

Right now I’m working at Patreon, where we spent the last year or so redesigning our product across platforms while updating our brand. I worked on many things as a part of that workstream, but am the most proud of the new podcast player

Before that, I spent a couple years at Apple working on Apple Pay and Wallet. I worked on Tap to Pay and Apple savings, among some other fun things I hope to see launch someday.

I like all parts of the design process, but I get the most energy from the fun technical stuff like motion design and prototyping. Over my career I’ve worked in a few different industries, and I think learning new spaces is part of what makes design so fun.

At this point in my career, I’m most interested in working on smaller teams to solve bigger problems. I like wearing multiple hats and taking ideas from 0 to 1. I also enjoy advising and mentoring smaller teams and new designers.
